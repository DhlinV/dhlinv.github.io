Honglin He

About me

I am a visiting researcher at the Department of Computer Science, University of California, Los Angeles, advised by Bolei Zhou.

I obtained my Master (2021.09 - 2024.06) at the Department of Automation, Tsinghua University, advised by Professor Yi Zhang and Assistant Professor Shuo Feng. Before that, I received my B.Eng (2017.09 - 2021.06) at the Department of Automation, Xiamen University, advised by Associate Professor Xunyu Zhong.

My research interest typically lies in Generative AI, Neural Rendering, Human-centric 3D topics, Embodied AI and Autonomous Driving.
