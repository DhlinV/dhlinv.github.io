<h2 style="margin-top: 50px;">About me</h2>
I am a visiting researcher at the Department of Computer Science, University of California, Los Angeles, advised by <a href="https://boleizhou.github.io/">Bolei Zhou</a>. 

I obtained my Master (2021.09 - 2024.06) at the Department of Automation, Tsinghua University, advised by Professor Yi Zhang and Assistant Professor <a href="https://www.fshuo.tech/">Shuo Feng</a>. Before that, I received my B.Eng (2017.09 - 2021.06) at the Department of Automation, Xiamen University, advised by Associate Professor Xunyu Zhong.

My research interest typically lies in <strong>Generative AI</strong>, <strong>Neural Rendering</strong>, <strong>Human-centric 3D topics</strong>, <strong>Embodied AI</strong> and <strong>Autonomous Driving</strong>.
